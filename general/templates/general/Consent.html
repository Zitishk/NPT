Procedure

Participants will have to make an incentivized choice in 11 rounds of an economic experiment. Depending on the choices they make they will be paid money. Then they will be asked to write a short survey. The total time to complete the session will be about 1.5 hours.

Risks

There are no risks of participation in the study. The participants may discontinue the research participation at any time during the study without any penalty.

Benefits

Participation in this study is voluntary and they will be paid a small sum of money for participating in the experiment. Paying money for choices made in an experiment is an internationally accepted convention in Economics. Participants are free to leave the session at any point during the study.

Right to Information

Participants are free to ask any questions during/after the data collection process.

Confidentiality and Privacy

Privacy and Confidentiality will be maintained. The responses will be anonymized using a subject code for each participant. Only de-identified data will be shared for analysis by the researchers.
